About Kingdom Support Initiatives(KSI)

Kingdom Support Initiatives is a God-ordained commission for raising God-centred generation who will impact the world with the new life that is in Jesus Christ. Through our initiatives, we reaffirm God- consciousness in our everyday life and create platforms for disciples to connect, interact and strengthen one another to grow in God. Like Aaron and Hur who held Moses’ hands, we hold others’ hands by servicing kingdom projects, ideas and callings through diverse opportunities.

KSi started in 2014 with our daily devotional and subsequently Kingdom Life Summit. We are a team of God-loving brethren whom God has called and earnestly pray, ‘Thy kingdom come and Thy will be done on earth as it in heaven’ (Matthew 6:10).

At Kingdom Support Initiatives (KSi), we are called to:
Service God’s bride to build a generation that truly know God. Support kingdom partners in the execution of their God given ideas to impact the world.

Slogan: raising a God-conscious this generation

Our Key Initiatives

‘In His Footstep’

‘In His Footstep’ is a daily devotional designed for readers who wants to build a continuous relationship with God. Through the word of God and the power of the Holy Spirit, the devotional seeks to help readers draw closer to God daily and raise a God-conscious generation.


Inspire Impact
It is a podcast in the making to encourage and inspire God’s children to be bold and pursue whatever God is directing them to do. We believe as Christians, we are to make impact and make heaven, that’s what we do!

Kingdom Life Summit
It is an annual retreat to refresh, re-evaluate and re-strategize in God’s presence. At the retreat, we spend time to pray, seek counsel from one another on God’s kingdom projects for the coming year. Since 2015, Kingdom Life Summit holds at the Redemption Camp in Nigeria.
